Welcome to Social Stories: COVID-19 Edition!

We are two Yale students who created this website in order to help children adjust to the new social expectations and routines which the coronavirus has created this year. We understand that many children, especially those in the autism community, face extraordinary challenges during this crisis, and we hope that these stories can be a resource for anyone struggling to adjust to these new circumstances.

The Social Stories concept was developed by child pediatrician Dr. Carol Gray in the early 1990s. A social story is a narrative made to illustrate certain situations and problems and how people deal with them. They help children with autism understand social norms and learn how to communicate with others appropriately.

We based the interactive social stories in this website off of social stories developed by Autism Little Learners and Tara Tuchel. Please refer to her website for more social stories and more resources for the autism community: www.autismlittlelearners.com

Please use our Contact Us page to get in touch with us! We'd love to hear from you.
